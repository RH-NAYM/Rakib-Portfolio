About

Engineering Philosophy

I build AI systems that operate in production—not experiments, not proofs of concept, but infrastructure that organizations depend on. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, distributed systems, and reliability engineering.

Technical Background

Over a decade of experience building systems that process millions of requests, manage petabytes of data, and maintain uptime requirements that don't forgive shortcuts. Deep expertise in Python, Rust, and C++ for performance-critical components.

Current Focus

The emergence of large language models has created new challenges in serving infrastructure. Current work focuses on efficient LLM deployment: continuous batching, KV-cache management, speculative decoding, and cost optimization.
