Technical Background

Over a decade of experience leading AI projects that process millions of requests, handle terabytes of data, and maintain near-zero downtime. Projects include real-time face verification, speech recognition, AI-based fire and accident detection, and satellite image analysis for environmental monitoring.

Expertise in Python for AI/ML pipelines, FastAPI and Django for production-grade API and web service development, and deep learning frameworks including TensorFlow and PyTorch for computer vision and NLP. Proficient with OpenCV for image/video processing, Pandas for data analysis, and GPU acceleration using CUDA and TensorRT. Experienced with distributed computing frameworks like Ray, Spark, and Flink, and deploying scalable AI systems on Kubernetes and cloud infrastructure across AWS, GCP, and Azure.

Current Focus

Scaling AI systems for real-world applications, including large language model serving, computer vision pipelines, real-time inference, and custom speech recognition systems. Key areas of focus include asynchronous and multithreaded deployments, continuous batching, KV-cache management, speculative execution, hyper-parameter optimization, and cost optimization across heterogeneous GPU and CPU hardware. Goal: deliver advanced AI capabilities that are reliable, high-performance, cost-effective, and operationally sustainable in production environments.
